Girl Scout Pie*

14 Thin Mint Cookies
3 Egg Whites
dash (1/16 tsp) of salt
3/4 cup sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/2 to 1 cup sweetened whipped cream

Chill cookies in refrigerator before using.
Crush cookies between wax paper with rolling pin,
roll until crumbs are even. Beat egg whites until
soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar, beating until
peaks become stiff. Fold in cookie crumbs, nuts,
and vanilla and spread in buttered 9 in pie plate.
Bake at 325* for 35 min; cool thoroughly. Top with
sweetened whipped cream (when thick, add 2 tsp sugar
and 2 drops vanilla); chill for 3-4 hours and garnish
with shaved chocolate just before serving.

*Disclaimer: not made with real Girl Scouts
